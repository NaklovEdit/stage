What is CoESIUM?

CoESIUM (Center of Excellence for a Sustainable Industry at UTP & Arts et Métiers) is a strategic partnership platform between Universiti Teknologi PETRONAS in Malaysia and the Arts et Métiers school (ENSAM) in France. Our mission is to accelerate international cooperation, drive student mobility, and foster joint research projects focused on sustainable development and the industry of the future.

Arts et Métiers (ENSAM)

As a prestigious French engineering school, Arts et Métiers trains responsible engineers capable of designing sustainable innovations. With multiple campuses across France, it brings cutting-edge expertise in mechanical, industrial, and energy engineering to the CoESIUM partnership.

Discover more

Universiti Teknologi PETRONAS

Ranked among the top 250 universities globally, UTP is a leading private university in Malaysia owned by PETRONAS. It offers a highly international environment with students from over 50 countries and world-class research facilities, making it a perfect hub for technological advancement.

Student Mobility & Research

CoESIUM acts as a bridge for students and researchers. We provide opportunities for exchange programs, dual PhD degrees, and joint funding for innovative research in smart manufacturing, renewable energies, and environmental management.

Key Collaboration Areas

Industrial Decarbonisation & Green Hydrogen

Joint research projects supported by CETIM, Arts et Métiers, and UTP are actively focusing on green hydrogen storage, additives evaluation, and hybrid AI models for non-destructive vision-based defect testing to optimize modern clean energy infrastructures.

Research Overview

Immersive Technologies & VR Rehabilitation

Through cotutelle doctoral research and internships at Lab Lispen, CoESIUM leads advancements in haptic VR for crane operations and real-time adaptive stroke rehabilitation systems (ADAPT-MOVE), creating smart, human-centered assistive technologies.
